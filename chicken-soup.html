Chicken Soup recipe

This creamy chicken soup is the perfect cold weather soup. Thick and hearty, it comes together fast and is perfect in bread bowls…or not! I tried to come up with a million other creative names for this soup. But eventually I abandoned the effort. Why? Because this creamy chicken soup is so amazing, it doesn’t need a fancy name to prove it.


  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of carrots
  • 1/2 cup of celery
  • 2 cubes of chicken bouilion
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 3 teaspoons of flour
  • 4 cups of cooked chopped chicken

cooking steps

  1. Use a pan
  2. add olive oil until simmer
  3. add all Ingredients
  4. Enjoy
